Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we will gladly accept your return or exchange within 2 days of purchase.

To apply for a refund request, please contact our customer service team at Support Server or support@sprucebot.ml with your order number and reason for the refund.

After a successfull refund your @prime member of our support server , and all prime features will be removed from your account and the prime server. you'll not able to request refund after passing 2 days of purchase.

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days of receipt of the returned item. Exchanges will be processed within 3-5 business days of receipt of the returned item.

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact our customer service team.
